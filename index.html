About osu!droid

osu!droid is an unofficial port of osu for android. This game only has one mode, this is osu!standard. The main goal of a player is to achieve more score and get to the top of the leaderboard for the most score. Instead of osu! performance points (pp), the developers had made a new points currency, which is the droid performance points, aka dpp, but the calculation is very different by its differences by ~ 37%
